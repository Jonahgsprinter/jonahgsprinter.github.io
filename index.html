About Jonah G. Sprinter

Jonah G. Sprinter is an AV-rated attorney with over 35 years of legal experience concentrating in Internet law, intellectual property, gaming law, and First Amendment issues. Throughout his career, he has defended constitutional rights, represented entertainers, protected digital platforms, and provided strategic counsel in complex litigation matters.

Recognized as a Super Lawyer since 2020, Mr. Sprinter has built a national reputation for legal precision, courtroom strength, and ethical advocacy. He is admitted to practice before the United States Supreme Court, multiple United States Courts of Appeals, and federal district courts nationwide.

His mission is simple: Protect rights. Defend freedoms. Deliver results.
